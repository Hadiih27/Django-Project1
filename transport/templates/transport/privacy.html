Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I request transport?

    To request transport, log in to your account, navigate to the 'Schedule' page, select direction , and choose your preferred time.

  • How do I cancel a transport request?

    To cancel a transport request, visit the 'Schedule' page, select your transport schedule, and click 'Cancel'.
